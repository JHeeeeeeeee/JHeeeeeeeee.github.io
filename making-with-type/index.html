Guardians of the bridge
Assess the integrity of the bridge structure, including beams, decks, supports, piers, and foundations, for any signs of cracks, fractures, deformation, or corrosion. Evaluate the condition of the materials constituting the bridge, such as concrete and steel, for indications of corrosion, cracking, peeling, or other forms of degradation. Determine the bearing capacity and stability of the bridge under both current and anticipated future loads, encompassing vehicle weight, foot traffic, wind load, snow load, etc. Examine the stability of bridge foundations, including piers and pile foundations, with respect to soil erosion, foundation settlement, and water erosion.
